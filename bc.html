BUSINESS CASES

1. Listing of All Preferences of Users The system allows users to add, update, and display their preferences,
such as favorite destinations or activities, with options for sorting or categorizing them.

2. Tourist Package Users can view and sort tourist packages based on price, allowing them to select options within their budget.

3. Tourist Attraction The system displays tourist attractions and sorts them based on their rank or popularity, helping users prioritize top-ranked attractions.

4. Transportation Mode The system shows available transportation modes (e.g., car, bus, train) for traveling outside the city, offering the option to choose the most suitable one.

5. Listing All the Carpooling Options The system matches users with potential carpool partners based on similar travel routes, facilitating shared transportation.

6. Listing the Timings (Arrival/Departure) The system sorts transport timings (e.g., buses, trains, flights) based on arrival or departure times, making it easier for users to plan their trips.

7. Shop Local The system displays local shops, sorted alphabetically or by category, promoting local culture and encouraging users to shop locally.

8. Emergency Help The system provides a list of nearby emergency services (e.g., hospitals, police stations) based on the user's current location for quick assistance in emergencies.
