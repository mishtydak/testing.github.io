OVERVIEW

The Travel Management System is designed to make travel planning easy, efficient, and personalized. It helps users organize their trips by offering features like sorting preferences, finding nearby attractions, eco-friendly transport options, and even a list of emergency services. With smart algorithms like Dijkstraâ€™s for finding the quickest routes and simple sorting techniques for organizing options, the system ensures that everything is just a few clicks away. This project is all about creating a seamless and enjoyable travel experience while keeping sustainability and convenience in mind.

PROBLEM SPACE

Travel planning can be stressful and time-consuming, with challenges like managing preferences, finding nearby attractions, eco-friendly options, and emergency services. Many existing tools focus on bookings but lack personalization and support for sustainable, localized travel experiences.

HOW IT WAS ARRIVED

While going on a trip, we realized the need for a platform that simplifies planning while prioritizing convenience, sustainability, and safety. This inspired us to create a solution that combines smart algorithms with a user-friendly design to make travel seamless and enjoyable.
